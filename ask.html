ASK PEER COUNSELING
As an ASK Peer Counselor, I was trained to assist students in the College of Letters & Science with questions about rules & regulations, deadlines, referrals, petitions, and more. I provided face-to-face advising with over 40 undergraduates per day by constructing multi-step strategies to solve the student's problem at hand. In addition, I assist in tracking the time, topic and location of the question asked to collect data on how undergraduates interact with ASK Peer Counselors in order to make future improvements.

Furthermore, I presented workshops for undergraduates on behalf of the counseling department on various topics ranging from degree requirements for graduation to commencement ceremony details. I am also involved in the competitive selection process for ASK Peer Counselors of the upcoming year.
