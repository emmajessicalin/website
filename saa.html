STUDENT ALUMNI ASSOCIATION
A student volunteer group established in 1984, the Student Alumni Association (SAA) works on behalf of the UCLA Alumni Association to facilitate interaction between students and alumni, and to enhance the student experience by providing opportunities that strengthen their lifelong loyalty to UCLA.

Public Relations Executive Committee

As the Social Media Director, I managed official social media platforms, created unique digital content, and brainstormed and executed new marketing projects.

Our focus, as the Public Relations Executive Committee, was to organize marketing campaigns for each event by encompassing community outreach, social media and alumni engagement. We were responsible for communicating the mission of SAA and the Alumni Association to the student body.
