Director of Marketing

As Director of Marketing (Summer/Fall 2016), I designed all marketing materials for fall recruitment 2016, including flyers, apparel and online graphics.

I also established a tracking and point system to keep over 50 members accountable for marketing efforts on-campus. Each member was given a number of points to reach as a goal, depending on their position within the fraternity. They would be awarded different numbers of points for their individual marketing efforts, such as sharing posts on social media, going to different classrooms to make announcements or handing out flyers. This recruitment was one of the most successful ever, with over 100 students attending the first day of the recruitment week.
